<analysis>**original_problem_statement**: The user wants to create an automatic invoice generator for their business, Thryve Coworking.

This evolved into a full-stack application with the following requirements:
-   **Core Functionality**: Generate invoices with client details, invoice number, date, and line items with GST calculation.
-   **PDF Export**: Export invoices as PDF files.
-   **Payment Tracking**: Track invoice payment status (Pending, Paid) and due dates.
-   **Bulk Operations**: Generate invoices in bulk by uploading an Excel file.
-   **Sequential Numbering**: Auto-incrementing invoice numbering system.
-   **Authentication**: Role-based access for admins.
-   **Coworking Management System**: Expand the app to manage members, plans, pricing, meeting room bookings, support tickets, and community announcements.
-   **Member Termination**: Functionality to deactivate members (individually or by company) while preserving their history for auditing.
-   **Member Portal**: A secure, separate portal for coworking members to log in and view their own invoices, bookings, and support tickets.

**User's preferred language**: English

**what currently exists?**
A full-stack application (React frontend, FastAPI backend, MongoDB) that serves as a comprehensive coworking space management tool.

-   **Admin Panel**: A secure section for staff to manage the entire system.
    -   **Authentication**: A complete role-based authentication system for admins is in place.
    -   **Invoicing**: Full-featured invoice generation (single, bulk from Excel), PDF export, and payment tracking.
    -   **Management**: Modules for managing Members, Plans (with flexible pricing), Meeting Room Bookings, Support Tickets, and Announcements.
    -   **Member Termination**: Allows admins to terminate members individually or in bulk by company, with options to track reasons and outstanding dues, while preserving member history.
    -   **Dashboard**: A revamped central dashboard with an icon-based UI providing quick access to all major functions and an overview of key business metrics.

-   **Member Portal**: A separate, secure, client-facing portal.
    -   **Authentication**: Members can register (only if their email exists in the admin-managed member list) and log in. This uses a separate JWT authentication flow from the admin panel.
    -   **Features**: Members can view their personal dashboard, see their invoice history, check booking schedules, manage their support tickets, and view announcements.
